How can we help?

Find answers to common questions about Sripuram One.

How do I book an accommodation?
Navigate to the Accommodations section in the app, select your dates, choose an available room type, and proceed to payment. You will receive a confirmation once the booking is successful.
Can I cancel my Seva booking?
Yes, cancellations are allowed up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Please visit the "My Bookings" section to initiate a cancellation.
What payment methods are supported?
We support all major Credit/Debit Cards, UPI, Net Banking, and Wallets through our secure Razorpay integration.
I'm facing an issue with the app. Whom do I contact?
For any technical issues or booking discrepancies, please email us at support@sripuram.org or call our toll-free number provided in the app's Help section.
