Privacy Policy

Request Account Deletion

If you no longer wish to use the Sripuram ONE application, you can request to have your account and associated data deleted.

Warning: Deleting your account is a permanent action. You will lose access to your profile, saved details, booking history, and all associated data within the Sripuram ONE ecosystem. It may take up to 30 days for all data to be permanently erased from our backups.

Please fill out the form below to initiate the account deletion process.

Alternatively, Delete in the App

You can also delete your account instantly from within the Sripuram ONE mobile app by navigating to:
Profile → Settings → Delete Sripuram ONE account.
